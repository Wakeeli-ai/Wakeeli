## Research Summary

After exhaustive searching across all individuals in sections 3 and 4 of the master list, **confirmed active or semi-active personal X/Twitter accounts are extremely rare** in this segment of the Lebanese real estate market. Most agents, brokers, consultants, and sales managers in Lebanon maintain presence on Instagram, LinkedIn, and WhatsApp rather than X/Twitter. The handful of confirmed handles are documented below.

**Note on verification limitations:** X/Twitter blocks JavaScript-based content scraping, which means tweet dates and exact post counts could not be retrieved directly. Activity levels are assessed based on indirect evidence: handle confirmation in search indexes, cross-referencing with other platform activity, and recent web mentions.

## Key Takeaways

1. **Extremely low X/Twitter adoption** across this segment of Lebanese real estate professionals. Out of 67 individuals searched, only 2 unverified personal handles were found and 1 dormant company account.

2. **Instagram and LinkedIn dominate** for this market segment. Nearly every professional who has social presence uses Instagram and LinkedIn, not X/Twitter.

3. **Only confirmed handles (unverified activity):**
   - Bilal Faour (@BilalFaour_): Coldwell Banker, UAE-based Lebanese RE professional
   - Charbel Choueh (@charbelchoueh): Real estate lawyer, fintech/crypto expert, active in regional events in 2025

4. **X/Twitter outreach is not recommended** for this bottom half of the leads list. Instagram DMs and LinkedIn messages are far more likely to reach these professionals.

5. **Contrast with top half:** The top half (owners/founders/CEOs/directors) is likely to have more X presence due to higher public profiles and industry commentary roles.
