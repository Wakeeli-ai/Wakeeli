## Part 7: The Guarantee

### Option A: The Performance Guarantee (Recommended)

> **"If Wakeeli doesn't book more property tours in your first 30 days than the 30 days before, we refund 100% of your first month's subscription fee. No questions asked."**

This is the right guarantee because:
- It's tied to the outcome they actually care about (tours, not technology)
- The baseline is "more than last month" -- an achievable bar
- It works with almost every agency because most agencies have inconsistent tour booking
- It forces Fox to onboard well (good for retention)
- In Lebanon where trust is everything, this removes every remaining objection

### Option B: The First Tour Guarantee (Simpler)

> **"If Wakeeli doesn't book at least one qualified property tour for your agency in the first 30 days, you pay nothing."**

This is easier to explain and even lower friction. Use this one with more skeptical prospects.

### The Logic Behind the Guarantee

Hormozi's principle: stack the risk on yourself to eliminate every objection from the buyer. If the product works, you almost never pay out the guarantee. You signal confidence by offering it, which alone increases close rates.

With Wakeeli, the guarantee is virtually zero risk because:
1. Every agency will get at least one tour booked through AI in 30 days (the system is always on)
2. The "more than last month" bar is low -- most agencies have weak follow-up currently
3. Any agency skeptical enough to invoke the guarantee was probably not a long-term client anyway
